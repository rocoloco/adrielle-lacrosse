About Me

I was born in Florida and moved to Madrid only eight months ago. Lacrosse is not a common sport here, and it is even less common within the Dominican community I grew up around, so I did not actually find the game until two years ago. Once I picked up a stick the passion took hold immediately and it has not let up since. I play midfield for Madrid Osos and I also play for the Dominican Republic national team when they are in season. Soccer has been part of my life just as long. I play for my school team, where I am captain, and for a club team as well. Chasing two sports at a high level across two countries has taught me how to manage my time, stay disciplined, and compete no matter who I am lined up against.
